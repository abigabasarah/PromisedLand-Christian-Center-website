Our Ministries

Discover opportunities to serve, grow, and connect at Promised Land Christian Center

Evangelism and Outreach

Through evangelism, we proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and share His message of hope and salvation with our community and beyond. Our Outreach Ministry extends God's love beyond our church walls by serving those in need, demonstrating Christ's compassion through practical acts of service, and supporting missions around the world.

What We Offer:

  • Charity outreach programs
  • Hospital and home visitations
  • Street evangelism programs

When We Meet

Wednesdays: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Location: Mainly at church or Varies by project

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry provides a safe, fun, and faith-filled environment where children can learn about God's love through age-appropriate lessons, worship, crafts, and games.

What We Offer:

  • Sunday School classes for ages 3-12
  • Sunday School classes for ages 13-17
  • Special events and holiday celebrations
  • Trained, background-checked , and caring Sunday School volunteers
  • Safe and secure check-in system
  • Musical Instrument Training

When We Meet

Sundays:10:00 AM - 12:30 PM(during main service)

Location:First Floor, Church Building

Worship Ministry

Our Worship Ministry leads the congregation in worship through gospel music, prayer, and heartfelt devotion, while equipping, nurturing, and mentoring members to grow spiritually and develop their God-given talents.

What we offer:

  • We nurture and develop singing talents for the glory of God and service in the church
  • We train instrumentalists to serve in praise and worship ministry
  • The Worship team conducts and leads annual Worship Sundays
  • Worship Minisstry Bible study and overnights

When We Meet

Every Saturday: 3:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Location: Church Building
