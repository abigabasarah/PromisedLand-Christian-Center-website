About Our Church

Our History

Plcc ministry begun on the knees of a woman, in her bedroom, on a small mat beside her bed some where around 2022.This woman, knelt and prayed to God every night & day for close to a year until the instruction came from on high to start what we now see as a big and growing ministry. From her bedroom rose a big prayer altar, starting with a few members (about 12) that later turned into Promised land Christian centre church.So church started in the living room of Mr and Mrs Hiire’s home. Ps Grace was the very first pastor of PLCC (at home). She also was the choir, the usher and the congregation was the empty chairs in the room.She kept prophesying people into those chairs and before long, it manifested. Her husband, who was a minister at a former church joined her and ministry took a whole different turn for the better.Families joined and before long, the house wasn’t enough to host the church. And then, there came PLCC
