New Here?

We're so glad you're considering visiting Promised Land Christian Center(PLCC)

Welcome!

We know visiting a new church can be intimidating. We want to make your first visit with us as comfortable as possible. Here's what you can expect when you join us for service.

No matter where you are in your spiritual journey, you're welcome here. Come as you are and experience the love of Christ through our church family.

What to Expect

Service Length

Our Sunday worship service typically lasts about 150 minutes. We start with praise and worship followed by biblical teaching and scripture based prayer.

DressCode

We welcome everyone to come as they are. Whether you prefer casual wear, jeans, or business casual attire, we encourage dressing in a comfortable, modest, and respectful manner suitable for church.

Children

We have excellent children's programs during Sunday school services. Your children will have fun while learning about Jesus and other biblical teachings in age-appropriate ways.

Parking

Ample parking is available within the church premises, with ushers on hand to guide and assist you as required,security personnel are present around the church grounds to help ensure a safe, convenient and welcoming environment for all.
